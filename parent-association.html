Parent Association (PA)

The Luanda International School’s Parent Association consists of parent volunteers working to promote school spirit and build close relationships with parents, faculty and students.

The PA works closely with the LIS administration to support our children’s education in a variety of ways. We raise funds to enrich the classroom experience, organize family social events and provide support for both teacher and student-led initiatives.

All parents are invited to attend our monthly meetings. Check out our Facebook Group and Instagram Profile for the latest information on our upcoming events.

Please feel free to contact us if you have any questions or suggestions. We look forward to meeting you and welcoming you to the LIS community!
